Key finding

Routine response time averages in prior backfill were understated because the metric counted multi-unit responses more than once per incident. One incident should produce one response time. Emergency and Urgent remained consistent.

Example

September 2025 Routine average changes from 1.10 minutes to 2.42 minutes after incident-level deduplication.

Decision requested

  • Approve full historical correction for Oct 2024 through Oct 2025.
  • Adopt incident-level deduplication for all future reporting.

Reporting note

Add a short methodology line on the dashboard stating multi-unit responses are deduplicated to one record per incident.

What changes in the numbers

  • Routine averages trend about 1.7 to 2.4 minutes by month, instead of 1.1 to 1.4.
  • Twelve of thirteen Routine months increase after deduplication.
  • Average increase across the 13-month window is about 61 percent.
